Our Products

The Orbitz is not just a tribute to the classic but an exploration of innovation and cultural fusion. We hope you find familiar flavors in the new Orbitz, along with new surprises.

Mango Maple Tea

combines the tropical sweetness of mango and lychee with the earthy richness of maple syrup, topped with delightful textures of chewy bubble tea pearls, tender coconut jelly, and herbal grass jelly.
Mango Maple Tea offers a refreshing taste while being mindful of your health, with its reduced sugar content enhancing the natural flavors of the fruit and maple syrup. Enjoy a guilt-free indulgence that supports your well-being without compromising on the delicious, tropical experience.

Lychee Maple Tea

blends the exotic, floral sweetness of lychee with the warm, earthy richness of maple syrup, enhanced by the delightful textures of chewy bubble tea pearls, tender coconut jelly, and herbal grass jelly.
This delectable blend not only satisfies your taste buds but also offers a refreshing alternative with lower sugar content, making it a healthier choice. Each sip is a harmonious fusion of vibrant lychee and smooth maple, providing a unique and satisfying treat that's both indulgent and nourishing.

Peach Maple Tea

blends the juicy, sun-kissed flavor of ripe peaches with the warm, earthy richness of maple syrup, enhanced by the delightful textures of chewy bubble tea pearls, tender coconut jelly, and herbal grass jelly.
This delightful blend not only tantalizes your taste buds but also serves as a refreshing choice with reduced sugar content, promoting a healthier lifestyle. Every sip is a perfect balance of luscious peach and rich maple, delivering a unique and gratifying experience that is both indulgent and wholesome.
